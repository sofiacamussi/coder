This is Sofia Camussi,

historian, activist, artist & creator...

My life's tapestry weaves together diverse disciplines, playfully uniting the past, present, and co-created futures shared with grassroots communities in the global South. At my core, I'm devoted to nurturing interconnected projects that transcend the limitations of current systems. My passion resonates in championing the construction of human and territorial/ecosystemic rights, envisioning holistic decolonization processes empowering our communities and honoring our planet. I aspire to catalyze societal transitions, fostering localized communities and fortifying resilient democratic frameworks to address the impending climate crisis in our anthropocene era. I engage in multiple facets of exploration, envisioning a world where we're not only responsible for the systems we create but also spiritually and communally equipped for self-liberation and the liberation of all beings.
